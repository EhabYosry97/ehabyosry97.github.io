Project Overview

The Industrial IoT PCB is a cutting-edge solution designed for manufacturing facilities to monitor production lines in real time. This device collects data such as equipment performance, environmental conditions, and operational metrics, transmitting it seamlessly to cloud platforms for actionable insights.

Technical Details

The PCB integrates advanced IoT technologies to ensure accurate data collection and efficient connectivity. Key specifications include:

  • Microcontroller: ARM Cortex-M4 for high-performance processing.
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi, GSM, or LoRa for versatile communication options.
  • Sensors: Integrated support for vibration, temperature, and humidity sensors.
  • Power Supply: Operates on a low-power design with battery backup.
  • Protocol Support: Compatible with MQTT, Modbus, and other industrial standards.
  • Cloud Integration: Built-in support for AWS IoT, Azure IoT Hub, or custom solutions.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Data Collection: Captures and transmits critical production metrics such as uptime, downtime, and performance.
  • Cloud Connectivity: Securely sends data to the cloud for storage, analysis, and visualization.
  • Wireless Communication: Provides flexible options for network connectivity, including Wi-Fi and GSM.
  • Industrial Protocol Integration: Seamlessly integrates with existing industrial systems through Modbus and MQTT protocols.
  • Robust Design: Engineered to withstand harsh industrial environments with a durable and weatherproof enclosure.
  • Energy Efficiency: Optimized for low power consumption, ensuring continuous operation in power-constrained environments.
